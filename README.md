# 🚨 Dispatcher Sim

**Your City. Your Decision.**

Sirens are wailing. An emergency call comes in. You are in the control center – and you make the decisions. Coordinate units in real-time on a living map, build your precinct, acquire vehicles, and master dynamic incidents – alone or with a real crew.

---

## 🎮 What is Dispatcher Sim?

Dispatcher Sim is a real-time emergency simulation game where **you** are the dispatcher. No scripts. No bots. Every call, every unit, every decision matters.

- Build and expand your own fire/rescue stations
- Recruit and manage real players as crew members
- Respond to organic, dynamic emergency calls generated by the living city
- Team up or go solo – strategy decides the outcome

Whether you prefer tactical solo play or high-stakes multiplayer coordination, every shift feels different.

---

## ✨ Key Features

**Build your precinct**  
Construct stations, purchase and upgrade vehicles. Your city grows with every smart decision you make.

**Real crew matters**  
Vehicles only roll when fully staffed by real players. Teamwork is not optional – it’s the difference between success and chaos.

**Dynamic emergency calls**  
Incidents emerge naturally from the world around you. Be ready for the unexpected.

**Team or risk?**  
Master missions alone or join forces with others. Pure strategy wins the day.
